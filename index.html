Turret Alarm Clock

A motorized alarm clock inspired by automated turrets. The system uses stepper motors and a servo to move and animate a turret that reacts when the alarm triggers. A DFPlayer Mini handles sound effects so the alarm feels like a small cinematic event instead of a simple beep.

  • Arduino based control with custom animation sequences
  • Stepper motors for turret rotation and elevation
  • Servo controlled motion for expressive movement
  • DFPlayer Mini for audio feedback and alarm sounds
  • Limit switches for homing and repeatable motion

Coin Counting Mechanism

A progression of prototypes to detect and count coins for a gamified experience. Different versions explored ultrasonic detection, a load cell for weight measurement and a stepper driven system to control coin flow.

  • Ultrasonic sensor (HC SR04) for detecting coin presence
  • Load cell version to measure weight and detect insertion
  • Stepper based control of coin slot and locking mechanism
  • Serial feedback for debugging and game style instructions

Manège tes méninges board game ecosystem

A complete set of 3D printed parts for an educational board game called "Manège tes méninges" used at Cégep de Jonquière. The project included design, iteration and production of multiple mechanical elements such as a ferris wheel and gear based pieces.

  • CAD modeling of all game components
  • Print tuning for durability and repeatability
  • Coordination with staff for quantities and deadlines
  • Delivery of full sets ready for classroom use

Vertical Hydroponic Tower and aquaponics work

A set of projects exploring how to grow plants in northern contexts using hydroponic and aquaponic systems. Includes a vertical tower design, experiments with nitrifying bacteria and tools for planning and analyzing system performance.

  • Design and construction of a vertical hydroponic tower
  • Aquaponic systems cycling with fish and plants
  • Data tracking and analysis of water quality and growth
  • Presentation at science fairs and public events
